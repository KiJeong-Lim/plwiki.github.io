증명보조기(proof assistant, interactive theorem prover)는 수학적 증명을 형식화하고 검사하기 위한 소프트웨어이다.

## 목록
* [Agda](Agda)
* [Coq](Coq)
* [Lean](Lean)
* [Abella](https://abella-prover.org/)
* [Arend](https://arend-lang.github.io/)
* [Beluga](https://complogic.cs.mcgill.ca/beluga/)
* [F*](https://fstar-lang.org/)
* [Idris](https://www.idris-lang.org/)
* [Isabelle](https://isabelle.in.tum.de/)
* [Matita](http://matita.cs.unibo.it/index.shtml)
* [Metamath](https://us.metamath.org/)
* [Narya](https://github.com/mikeshulman/narya)
* [RedPRL](https://redprl.org/)
* [Twelf](https://twelf.org/)
